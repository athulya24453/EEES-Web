ELECTROLIZER 2022s

Dec 31, 2022
The welcome for the E19 students in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering was held on the 31st of October from 4.30 pm onwards at Drawing Office 2. The new entrees to DEEE had the opportunity to interact with the academic staff and the evening was full of fun and enjoyment.

An Electrical Bike ride share program for in campus use

Oct 19, 2022
A webinar on an Electrical Bike ride share program for in-campus use was held on 19th October 2022 from 5.30 pm onwards via Zoom. The event was organized by the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Society in collaboration with the transportation committee of the Faculty of Engineering. The keynote speaker was Dr. Ruchira Dharmasena. He is a Process Engineer at General Motors Michigan, USA. The content of the session was the design of Electric bicycles, battery pack fabricating, Electric bicycle Engineering and understanding key elements.

Annual general meeting 2019-20

July 29, 2020
Annual general meeting of the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Society (EEES) was held on 29 th of July 2020, as a online event using Zoom due to the prevailing Covid 19 Pandemic situation of the country. The meeting was declared and welcomed by the President, Mr.Manuja Harasgama at 5.00 PM with the participation of the members.The Secretary of EEES, Mr.Ishan Rangajith presented the minutes of the previous Annual General Meeting.Then the junior treasurer of EEES, Mr. Thimila Bandara Ranaweera presented the financial report of the year 2018/19. After that former office bearers resigned from their posts, and then the new panels of office bearers were appointed. Dr. Roshan Godaliyadda was re-appointed as the senior treasurer of EEES.
